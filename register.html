Provide Bank Details

Provide your bank details, including your PAN and GSTIN. This is necessary so that Digikari can transfer your sales revenue to your bank account.

Pay Onboarding Charge

Pay the onboarding charge, a one-time fee that covers the cost of setting up your store on Digikari.
