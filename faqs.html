Who is Digikari suitable for?

Digikari is suitable for small businesses and homemade brands, particularly those who currently sell or plan to sell through platforms like Whatsapp, Instagram, and Facebook.

Will I be getting a Website through Digikari?

No. Through Digikari you create your own webstore/ecommerce store.

Does Digikari offer customer support?

Yes, Digikari offers customer support to help businesses set up and manage their online store. The platform also has a knowledge base with helpful articles and tutorials to help you get the most out of the platform.

Do I need any technical skills to use Digikari?

No, you don't need any technical skills to use Digikari. The platform has been designed to make it easy for anyone to use, regardless of their skill level or technical background.

I already have a website or I'm building one. Can Digikari be useful for me?

Yes, Digikari can still be useful for you even if you already have a website. If your website doesn't have e-commerce capabilities, you can create a webstore using Digikari and redirect your customers from your website to the store for purchases.

Will Digikari help me promote my brand or products to attract more customers?

No, Digikari does not offer promotional services. However, we have connections with partners who specialize in promotional activities and can assist in connecting you with them.

Is the initial registration cost a one-time payment?

Yes, the registration cost is a one-time payment. After that, you will only need to pay the monthly maintenance cost and bank charges for the payment gateway.

How long is my store valid after registration?

Your store has no expiration date or time limit after registration. You can use it for as long as you wish without any constraints.

Isn't it better to start selling online once I have more products?

Not necessarily. While having a wider range of products can be beneficial, it's not a requirement for starting an online business. In fact, starting with a smaller product line can help you focus on building a strong brand and establishing a loyal customer base. Additionally, starting small allows you to test the market and gather feedback, which can help you make better decisions about what products to offer in the future.
